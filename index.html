The Spark: What inspired my Coding Journey?

Posted on the 17th Feb. 2025

My name is Mcdavis Albertio, and I hold an HND in Administration and Finance, which I completed last year. However, after gaining practical experience in the field, I realized that it was not the right fit for me. While working in this domain, I often felt that I wanted to engage in more problem-solving tasks rather than solely performing administrative functions.

This realization led me to explore programming. I saw the potential to integrate programming with my background in Finance and Administration to develop solutions for financial challenges and automate repetitive tasks using modern technologies. With this in mind, I decided to embark on a journey to learn programming.

I began with Python, completing a foundational course on Coursera to grasp the basics. Recognizing the need for a more structured and immersive learning experience, I then set my sights on joining a coding boot camp to build a strong foundation in software development. Alongside Python, I committed to expanding my skills by delving into full-stack development. Currently, I am studying CSS and HTML through Khan Academy in preparation for admission into an advanced boot camp, which I plan to start in the coming weeks.

My learning path includes progressing to JavaScript next, ensuring that I am well-prepared for the intensive boot camp training ahead. My ultimate goal is to develop a solid technical foundation that will allow me to explore various aspects of the tech industry and leverage my programming skills to solve real-world financial and administrative problems.

Current Learning Path and Practice Approach

Posted on the 21st Feb. 2025

At this stage of my learning journey, I am focusing on strengthening my foundational web development skills. This week, I have dedicated several days off from work to refine my understanding of HTML and CSS. I am committing approximately four hours per day to hands-on practice, ensuring that I fully grasp the fundamental concepts.

My goal is to build a strong foundation in HTML and CSS before transitioning to JavaScript. By mastering these core technologies now, I aim to minimize the need for revisiting the basics later, allowing for a smoother learning experience as I progress. This structured approach ensures that I am well-prepared for the next phase of my programming journey.

Further updates on my progress and learning strategies will follow as I continue refining my skills and advancing towards my goals.

Turning Knowledge into Action

Posted on the 23rd Feb. 2025

As I move forward, my focus is on applying the skills I have gained to demonstrate my readiness for the next stage of my learning journey. While awaiting admission into an advanced boot camp, I plan to consolidate my knowledge of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript by building a fully functional webpage. This project will serve as both a practical application of my learning and a portfolio piece to showcase my capabilities.

My goal is to present this webpage to the institution as evidence of my progress, demonstrating my commitment and technical proficiency. By taking this approach, I aim to strengthen my candidacy for admission and ensure that I enter the program with a solid foundation, ready to take on more advanced challenges in programming.
